This planned lab is about helping content travel beyond the page where it was published.

The first version of the site already has a default Open Graph image, RSS, sitemap output, and metadata. Next I want to explore how those pieces can become more useful: better share previews, richer metadata, Markdown output, and formats that are easier for both people and AI tools to consume.
