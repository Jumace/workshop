# Vision

AI-assisted development made it easier for me to turn curiosity into something concrete. But it also made one thing clearer: the code is not the whole story.

The code matters. It shows the experiment, the implementation, the tradeoffs, and the limits. But the part I want to preserve is not only what I built. It is why I built it, what I was trying to understand, what surprised me, and what I learned from the process.

That is the reason this site is split into a blog and a lab.

The blog is for reflection. It is where I slow down and write about the questions behind the work.

The lab is for experiments. It is where I make the technical side visible: demos, setup notes, constraints, and things that are still rough around the edges.

Together, they give me a way to record both sides of learning in public: the thinking and the making.
